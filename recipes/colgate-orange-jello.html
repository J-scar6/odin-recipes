Colgate-Orange-Jello

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 cups orange juice
  • 2 envelope unflavored gelatin
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp citric acid
  • 4 tbsp colgate toothpaste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix orange juice and sugar until dissolved.
  2. In a separate bowl, dissolve gelatin in boiling water.
  3. Combine the gelatin mixture with the orange juice mixture.
  4. Add citric acid and colgate toothpaste, stirring well.
  5. Pour into molds and refrigerate until set.
