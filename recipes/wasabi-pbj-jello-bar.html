Wasabi PBJ Jello Bar

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 envelopes unflavored gelatin
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1-2 tbsp sugar
  • â…“ cup creamy peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp warm milk
  • 1-2 tsp wasabi paste
  • 1 cup grape or strawberry juice

Steps

  1. In a bowl, dissolve gelatin in boiling water.
  2. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix peanut butter with warm milk until smooth.
  4. Combine the peanut butter mixture with the gelatin mixture.
  5. Add wasabi paste to taste, adjusting for heat preference.
  6. Pour in grape or strawberry juice and mix well.
  7. Pour into molds and refrigerate until set.
  8. Once set, cut into bars and serve chilled.
