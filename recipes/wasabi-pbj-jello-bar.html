Wasabi PBJ Jello Bar

Description

Ingredients

  • 1Â½ cups dill pickle brine (from the jar)
  • 1 cup finely diced dill pickles
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1 cup vanilla pudding
  • crushed pretzels
  • 1 tbsp sugar

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix dill pickle brine and sugar until dissolved.
  2. In a separate bowl, dissolve vanilla pudding in boiling water.
  3. Combine the pudding mixture with the dill pickle brine.
  4. Add finely diced dill pickles, stirring well.
  5. Layer the mixture with crushed pretzels in a parfait glass.
  6. Refrigerate until set, then serve chilled.
